Principal's Message

"Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations".

Children are like buds in a garden and should be carefully and lovingly nurtured as they are the future of the nation and the citizens of tomorrow. “Aristotle once said “Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all”. We Pride ourselves to help our children to grow and develop into sensitive and responsible citizens of the next generation. A committed, caring and supportive management, dedicated teachers, cooperative parents blend harmoniously to create a child centric school. Real-life application of knowledge and skills has been the hallmark of the education revolution at SAMEER International Academy. 

In this SIA family, our students and teachers are the essential ingredients that allow our school to constantly evolve and to present society with strong, inventive and rational young people with well-defined personalities. Our earnest effort is to provide a conducive learning environment to each and every student so that when they go out of the portals, they brim with confidence and emerge as the leaders of tomorrow.

As your Principal, I thank everyone of you dear parents and well wishers for your continual support and collaboration in our service to the society..  We will strive to teach and provide students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset necessary to have a successful future. I am confident enough that the SIAians will make themselves stronger day by day, adding a new leaf to the grandeur of the school.

 Blessings & best wishes!
 Regards,

ASLAM KHAN
Principal, [NIT SCHOOL, DHO]]
